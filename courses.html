Programmes Offered

B.Ed & D.Ed Teacher Training Courses

Two-year, Osmania University–affiliated programmes designed to train confident, capable teachers for government and private schools across India.

Bachelor of Education

B.Ed Course

The B.Ed Programme aims to train teachers for schools in the government and private sector across India. Trainees build strong domain knowledge alongside practical teaching skills, with foundational and experiential coursework that prepares them to teach students of diverse linguistic, cultural, and learning backgrounds.

Duration
2 Years
Mode
Full-Time

Eligibility

  • 10+2 and Graduate, pass with 50% marks
  • Relaxation for SC / ST candidates as per Government norms
  • Relaxation for physically handicapped candidates as per Government norms

Diploma in Education

D.Ed Course

The D.Ed Programme aims to train teachers for schools in the government and private sector across India, with particular emphasis on primary-level teaching. The curriculum builds classroom-ready skills through child-centred teaching methods, structured pedagogy, and hands-on practical experience in real school settings.

Duration
2 Years
Mode
Full-Time

Eligibility

  • 10+2 pass with 50% marks
  • Relaxation for SC / ST candidates as per Government norms
  • Relaxation for physically handicapped candidates as per Government norms

What Makes Our Courses Different

Built Around Real Classroom Practice

Both programmes go beyond textbook theory, giving every trainee hands-on experience in relevant school settings throughout their studies.

🧩

Foundational Coursework

Core units in pedagogy, child development, and curriculum design form the base of both the B.Ed and D.Ed syllabus.

🏫

Trainees work in real school settings each semester, putting classroom theory into practice from early on.

📋

Research-Linked Projects

Diversity-Ready Skills
