About the College

Quality Teacher Education for Every Classroom

The B.Ed programme at Rabindranath Tagore College of Education trains teachers for government and private schools across India. Our curriculum and evaluation methods follow the standards used by leading Colleges of Education, with strong emphasis on building both subject knowledge and practical teaching skills.

Trainees complete foundational and experiential coursework that prepares them to teach students of diverse linguistic, cultural, learning, and behavioural backgrounds — with hands-on classroom experience in real school settings throughout the programme.

A Message from Our President

Our Vision for Quality Education

"There is a great need for quality teachers with knowledge of innovative teaching methodologies — and that is what Rabindranath Tagore College of Education undertakes."

Dr. Khadar Khan's vision is to provide quality education in India with special emphasis on primary education in both urban and semi-urban areas. Good primary education for our children — the future citizens of India — depends on having well-trained teachers equipped with modern, innovative methodologies. This belief shapes every aspect of our B.Ed and D.Ed programmes.
